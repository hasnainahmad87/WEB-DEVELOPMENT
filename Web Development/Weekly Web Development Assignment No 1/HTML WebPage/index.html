History of Football

Football, known as soccer in some countries, has origins dating back over 2,000 years to ancient China and Greece. However, the modern game was formalized in England in 1863 with the establishment of the Football Association (FA).

The first official international match was played in 1872 between England and Scotland. Today, football is the world's most popular sport, with the FIFA World Cup being the most-watched sporting event globally.

Basic Rules of Football

Football is played between two teams of 11 players each. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opponent's goal.

Key Rules:

  • Matches consist of two 45-minute halves
  • Only the goalkeeper can handle the ball within their penalty area
  • Offside rule prevents goal-hanging
  • Fouls result in free kicks or penalties
  • Yellow and red cards are used for disciplinary actions
